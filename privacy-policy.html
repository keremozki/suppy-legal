Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2025  ·  Effective: April 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how KOZKI ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Suppy mobile application ("App"). By using Suppy, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

Suppy is developed and operated by Kerem Özkırşehirli (KOZKI), based in Turkey. For any privacy-related questions, you can contact us at kerozkirsehirli@gmail.com.

2. Age Restriction

Suppy is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete that information.

3. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Information you provide directly:

  • Your name (optional, entered during onboarding)
  • Your supplement names, ingredients, dosages, and notes
  • Your daily supplement logs (taken or missed)
  • Your daily outcome check-ins (sleep, energy, mood, focus, recovery scores on a 1–5 scale)
  • Your profile photo (stored locally on your device only)

Information collected automatically:

  • An anonymous user identifier generated by Firebase Authentication when you first open the App
  • App usage events (such as onboarding completed, supplement added, streak milestones) collected through Firebase Analytics
  • Device type and operating system version (collected by Firebase)
  • Purchase and subscription status managed through RevenueCat

Information collected when you sign in:

  • If you choose to sign in with Google: your Google account name and email address
  • If you choose to sign in with Apple: your Apple ID name and email address (or the relay address Apple provides)
  • Sign-in is optional. The App functions without signing in.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the App's core features (supplement tracking, reminders, streaks, stats)
  • To show you pattern insights based on your own check-in and supplement data
  • To process subscription purchases and manage access to premium features
  • To send you local push notifications for supplement reminders (only if you grant permission)
  • To improve the App through aggregate, anonymised analytics
  • To respond to your support requests

5. Data Storage

Most of your personal data (supplements, logs, check-ins, name, theme preference) is stored locally on your device using your device's local storage. This data is not accessible by us unless you are signed in, in which case it is also synced to Google Cloud Firestore to allow data recovery.

Your profile photo is stored locally on your device only and is never uploaded to our servers.

Analytics data is processed by Google Firebase Analytics. Subscription data is processed by RevenueCat. Both are subject to their own privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share data only with the following service providers who help us operate the App:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and analytics (Firebase Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat — subscription and purchase management (RevenueCat Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign-In — optional authentication (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Apple Sign-In — optional authentication (Apple Privacy Policy)

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

7. Your Rights (KVKK & GDPR)

Under applicable data protection laws, including the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restriction — request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at kerozkirsehirli@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App's services. If you delete the App or request account deletion, we will delete your data from our servers within 30 days. Data stored locally on your device is deleted when you uninstall the App.

9. Push Notifications

Suppy uses local push notifications to remind you to take your supplements. These notifications are scheduled on your device and do not require your data to be sent to our servers. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings or within the App.

10. Children's Privacy

Suppy is not directed to children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

11. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
